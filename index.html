Ever wondered how you could make a difference in your community but stay close to home? Look no further than AmeriCorps! Founded in 1993 by President Bill Clinton, the goal of this government agency is meant to drive volunteerism and community service throughout the country. This program enthusiastically provides support and guidance to those in need and encourages understanding and camaraderie. With everything that is happening in the world today, why not start by making a difference right outside your front door?
